10 best youtube channels to learn coding in 2023

Author - Deepak Kumar
3 April. 5 min read

Coding has become one of the most important skills in the modern age. With the rise of technology and the internet, coding has become an essential tool for many industries. It’s a great skill to have if you’re looking to start a career in tech, or if you simply want to develop your problem-solving and logical thinking abilities.


One of the best ways to learn coding is through YouTube channels. There are countless channels out there that offer tutorials, advice, and examples of coding. In this blog post, we’ve compiled a list of the ten best YouTube channels for coding.


1. FreeCodeCamp.org : With over 5 million subscribers, FreeCodeCamp.org is one of the most popular coding channels on YouTube. They offer a wide range of tutorials and courses on programming languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

2. Traversy Media : Traversy Media is a channel dedicated to web development. They offer tutorials on a variety of topics, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and more. Their videos are easy to follow and perfect for beginners.

3. Programming with Mosh : Programming with Mosh is a channel that offers in-depth tutorials on a variety of programming languages, including C#, Python, and JavaScript. Their videos are perfect for intermediate and advanced coders looking to take their skills to the next level.

4. Derek Banas : Derek Banas is a veteran programmer with over 20 years of experience. His channel offers tutorials on a variety of programming languages, including Java, JavaScript, Python, and more. His videos are perfect for beginners and advanced coders alike.

5. The Net Ninja: The Net Ninja is a channel that offers tutorials on web development, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and more. Their videos are easy to follow and perfect for beginners.

6. Academind : Academind is a channel that offers tutorials on a variety of topics, including web development, machine learning, and more. Their videos are perfect for intermediate and advanced coders looking to expand their skillset.

7. CS Dojo : CS Dojo is a channel that offers tutorials on a variety of programming languages, including Python, Java, and more. Their videos are perfect for beginners and cover a wide range of topics.

8. TechLead : TechLead is a channel run by a former Google tech lead. Their videos offer insights into the tech industry, as well as tutorials on a variety of programming languages.

9. LearnCode.academy : LearnCode.academy is a channel that offers tutorials on web development, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and more. Their videos are easy to follow and perfect for beginners.

10. Codecourse : Codecourse is a channel that offers tutorials on web development, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and more. Their videos are perfect for beginners and cover a wide range of topics.




In conclusion, there are countless YouTube channels out there that offer tutorials and advice on coding. The ten channels listed above are some of the best out there and are perfect for beginners, intermediate, and advanced coders alike. Whether you’re looking to start a career in tech or simply want to develop your coding skills, these channels are a great place to start.
